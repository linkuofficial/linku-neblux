CONCEPT
Special Relativity
Physics
1905 · Albert Einstein

Light always travels at the same speed, no matter how fast you chase it — and to keep that one fact true, space and time themselves must stretch and bend.

The core idea

Moving clocks run slow and moving rulers contract, just enough to keep the speed of light fixed for every observer. There is no universal "now."

Why it matters

GPS satellites move fast enough that their clocks visibly drift from ours; without correcting for relativity, your phone's map would be off by kilometres within a day.
